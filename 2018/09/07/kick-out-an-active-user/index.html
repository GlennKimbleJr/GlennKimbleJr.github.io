Kick Out An Active User

September 7, 2018 by Glenn

I did a live stream the other day over a Larastream for a member who has requested an example of how to force one of his sites active users to logout.

The video is just a basic implementation to show the idea and get him started. This is by no means production ready code, as there are a lot of untested edge cases.

Watch the video over at Larastream or view the repo over on github.

I was having some trouble with my microphone (plus I have a mumbling problem in general) so it was a little hard to hear me. I'll try to do better on future videos.
